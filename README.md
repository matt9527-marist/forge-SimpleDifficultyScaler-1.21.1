## Simple Difficulty Scaler MC (SDS)

Presents a tiered difficulty system that is manually toggleable and increases the game's difficulty by tweaking the probabilities and spawn variety of the vanilla game, 
such as increasing the chances of mob spawns with gear, enchantments, and potion effects, introducing spawns of Strays, Bogged, and Husks outside of their intended
specific spawn locations, and adding modifiers that make survival combat against Minecraft mobs more challenging and interesting.

Difficulty Tiers 1-6
0) (normal Vanilla Minecraft)
1) Eclipse
2) Monsoon
3) Nightmare
4) Hell
5) Inferno
6) Torment

### Eclipse
+ chances to spawn Witches
+ chances for armored mobs up to iron
+ chances for enchanted mobs up to enchantment power 15
+ chances for mobs to spawn with weapons up to iron

### Monsoon
+ chance to spawn Pillagers
+ chances for armored mobs to spawn up to iron
+ chances for enchanted mobs from enchantment power 10-20
+ chances for mobs to spawn with weapons up to iron
  + Specific chance for mobs to spawn with diamond sword
+ chances for mobs to spawn with potion effects up to level 2

### Nightmare
+ chances to spawn Vindicators
+ chances to spawn Pillagers
+ chances for armored mobs to spawn up to diamond
+ chances for enchantd mobs from enchantment power 20-25
+ chances for mobs to spawn with weapons up to diamond
  + Specific chance for mobs to spawn with netherite sword
+ Piglin Brutes can now spawn in all Nether locations
+ Strays now spawn in all Overworld biomes
+ Husks now spawn in all Overworld biomes
+ Bogged now spawn in all Overworld biomes
+ chances for mobs to spawn with potion effects up to level 3

### Hell
+ chances to spawn Skeleton Knight
+ chances to spawn Spider Jockeys
+ Ravagers may now spawn naturally
+ chances to spawn Vindicators
+ chances to spawn Witches
+ chances to spawn Breezes
+ chances for armored mobs to spawn up to Netherite
+ chances for enchanted mobs to spawn from enchantment power 25-30
+ chances for mobs to spawn with weapons up to Netherite
+ chances for mobs to spawn with potion effects up to level 4
  + specific chance for mobs to spawn with Sharpness 5 Netherite Sword
  + specific chance for Johnny Vindicator to spawn with Sharpness 5 Axe
  + specific chance for Skeleton mobs to spawn with Flame Bow.
  + specific chance for Skeleton mobs to spawn with Power 5 Bow.
+ Mobs that can spawn with gear are now guaranteed to spawn with at least one piece of gear.
+ Size of naturally spawning Slimes

### Inferno
+ chances to spawn Skeleton Knight
+ chances to spawn Spider Jockeys
+ chances for mobs to spawn with oozing/Infested/weaving/wind charging potion effects
+ Piglins, Piglin Brutes can now spawn in the Overworld without zombification
+ Wither Skeletons can now spawn in the Overworld
+ chances to spawn Breezes
+ chances for Strays, Husks, and Bogged to spawn in place of Skeletons
+ Creepers can now naturally spawn charged
+ Size of naturally spawning Slimes

### Torment
+ chances to spawn ravagers increased
+ All Vindicators spawn with Johnny tag and Sharpness 5 Axe
+ Ravagers can spawn with Evokers riding them
+ all mobs that can spawn with gear must spawn with a full set of armor ranging from diamond to Netherite
+ chances for enchanted mobs to spawn with enchantment power 30
  + Specific chance for mobs to spawn with Sharpness 5 Knockback 2 Fire Aspect 2 Netherite Sword
  + Specific chance for Skeleton mobs to spawn with Power 5 Punch 2 Flame 1 Bow
  + All Pillagers will spawn with Quick Charge 6 Multishot Crossbows
+ all mobs that can spawn with a weapon are guaranteed to spawn with a weapon ranging from diamond to Netherite
+ chances for mobs to spawn with potion effects up to level 5
+ chances for mobs to spawn with oozing/infested/weaving/wind charging effects
+ Mobs can now spawn with a totem of undying in their offhand
+ Heavily increased chance for Piglin Brutes to spawn in place of Piglins in the Overworld without zombification
+ Wardens can spawn naturally above ground
